Household gods.

Old houses.

Appearance:

As any other household gods, it is invisible for people, but can be seen by the human eye in other forms, such as: dog, cat, gray old man, man, haystack, pig, family member. Nobody from human beings actually knows how it looks like!

Key Characteristics:

  • Invisible.
  • Can reincarnate into other objects.
  • Loves domestic animals!

What we know so far about domovoy?

Domovoy, according to the beliefs of the Slavs, is a good or evil spirit in the house, who is the actual “owner” of the house. The Domovoy is believed to protect the well-being of a kin in any of its aspects. It is very protective towards the children and the animals of the house, constantly looking after them. Domovoy is believed to share the joys and the sorrows of the family, and warn about future events. The Domovoy become angry and reveal their demonic aspect if the family is corrupted by bad behaviour and language.


Guidelines for human interaction with this creature:

Nobody knows how Domovoy looks like, and will never know since it is a spirit. However, thanks to its special love to animals, we can know whether it is present in the house or not. It can posses different animals. Try to find out which form Domovoy manifested in this time. When interacting with the animal, it will leave the appearance of the animal and fades away. But don't piss it off. It doesn't like to be disturbed by people.
